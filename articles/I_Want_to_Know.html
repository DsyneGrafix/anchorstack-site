Common Struggles Creators and Doers Share

Over the years, I’ve seen patterns emerge. Here are a few that show up over and over:

  • Lack of clarity: Not sure what to work on first—or why it matters.
  • Overthinking: Getting lost in loops and second-guessing everything.
  • Scattered focus: Too many projects pulling attention in every direction.
  • Imposter syndrome: Feeling like you're not ready, qualified, or worthy.
  • Burnout: You're doing all the things but feeling nothing in return.

If any of these resonate, you’re not alone. But naming your struggle is more than just venting— it’s the first step in regaining momentum.
